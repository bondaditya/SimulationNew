Be incisive

Remember, you can only change production from suppliers that you selected at the beginning of the year, you cannot begin production with a supplier once the production schedule has started. Go ahead and place your orders in the Production Room.

Lead Time

Keep in mind that the Lead Time will affect the availibilty of the product to the consumers. You need to be making a decision based on the quality. Saving on cost is the prime objective.

Capacity

If the vendor does't offer enough capacity you'll have to choose a different vendor.

Starting Month

Not every month will have the same sales growth so make your decision with starting month of the vendor in mind. Plan ahead.
